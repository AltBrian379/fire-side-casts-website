About Fireside Casts

Fireside Casts is an esports broadcasting organization that originated back in Mid-2020 for RGL TF2 Traditional Sixes. Since then, Fireside Casts has made its foray into many esports titles, including:

  • Team Fortress 2
  • Counter Strike Global Offensive
  • Pokemon
  • Rainbow Six Siege
  • Valorant

Fireside made its Valorant debut on November 6, 2022, covering the ZOTAC Cup NA Community Tournament 5v5 hosted by VNM Tournaments.
